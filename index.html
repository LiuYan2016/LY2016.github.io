Enjoy five-star luxury and complete privacy during your visit to Chiang Mai

Three Kings Villa is a luxury five-bedroom villa set in 8000 square meters of fenced-in gardens. The villa represents a total escape from hustle and bustle of Chiang Mai city, yet the location is close enough to town to be able to go in for daily visits.

The villa was constructed with modern comforts in mind yet still retains a distinct Thai feel. Natural light is in abundance, yet clever architecture means the house always remains cool. The views from the ground floor and various balconies are spectacular, with countryside and rolling hills as far as the eye can see.

The villa is a family home designed to delight all ages. A special feature of the villa is its movie room, which was built to offer a truly immersive experience with impressive sound & visuals.

The property lies in a particularly green part of Chiang Mai and borders the heavily forested hills towards Si Lanna National Park. Five minutes on a bicycle and you’re in the wilderness.

Three Kings Villa has over 1000m2 of living space and sleeps a total of 16 people.

Living Area & Kitchen

The main living area is bright and cool, and brings the garden in rather than shut it out. Excellent views towards the greenery, pool and hills beyond. The fully equipped kitchen and breakfast bar are directly connected to the living area, making the area ideal for families and groups.

Bed & Bathrooms

With two bedrooms on the ground floor and three upstairs, the villa sleeps a total of 16.

  • Executive master bedroom (two kingsize beds) with ensuite bathroom
  • VIP bedroom 1 (one kingsize bed and additional single bed) with ensuite bathroom
  • VIP bedroom 2 (one kingsize bed and additional single bed) with ensuite bathroom
  • Standard bedroom (one kingsize bed and additional single bed) with shared bathroom
  • Family bedroom (four single beds) with shared bathroom

Outdoor Areas & Pool

The exceptionally large saltwater spillover pool and deck make a perfect spot to enjoy a lazy day. Massages can be had in the sala or wake up with an early morning yoga session on the grass. At night, enjoy a BBQ under the stars.

Entertainment

A dedicated entertainment room means you’ll never have a boring day at Three Kings Villa. Shoot some pool, watch a movie or play some music, the choice is yours. Take your movie experience to the next level in the movie room, featuring unbelievable sound and picture quality.

Location

Bike Riding

There are some excellent roads around the villa, ideal for cycling. Traffic is low and as you get nearer the mountains the climbs become challenging.

Golf

With Mae Jo golf course at your doorstep and a further 11 world-class golf facilities in the near vicinity, the villa is perfect for a golf getaway.

Hiking

The hiking options around the villa are endless. Various walking tracks ply the hills to the East of the villa, meaning you’ll never have to walk the same route.

Fishing

With Mae Kuang reservoir nearby, you’ll have the option to fish to your heart’s content. Or try your luck on Huai Cho reservoir only 5 minutes from the villa.

Elephant Tours

Visit the nearby hill tribes, ride an elephant or take a Thai cooking course. Whatever the activity, we will happily advise and organize.

Spa & Massage

The spa at nearby Mae Jo Golf Course is world-class. Alternatively, staff can be brought in from outside to pamper you.

Yoga

Find a spot in the expansive garden for your yoga routine or have a professional yoga teacher join you at the villa.

City Excursions

Temple-city Chiang Mai is in easy reach and we’ll happily organize transport to and from. Foodie? Chiang Mai has exceptional dining options too.

Frequently Asked Questions

Rates

The base rate for the villa is ฿ 18,000 per night if your party consists of 12 people or less. For every extra guest over 12 we charge an additional ฿ 1,000 per person per night. With a party of 12 or more the price for the villa comes down to ฿ 1,500 per person per night or less! Maximum occupancy is 16 and the minimum duration of stay is 4 nights.

Staffing

The villa is managed by the owner, Roger. Please don’t hesitate to talk to Roger to discuss any specific requirements. The villa is fully staffed and your room will be attended to daily. We want to make your stay as care-free as possible!

Private chef

Three Kings Villa has a private chef who will happily prepare Western, Asian, vegetarian and children’s meals. Guest may choose to prepare their own meals using either of the two fully-equipped kitchens.

Food & Beverage

We will make sure you arrive in a fully stocked villa. To help us serve you better we ask you to fill in a ‘guest preference sheet’ before you arrive. This helps us set the villa up to your preferences. Non-alcoholic drinks, coffee, tea, and snacks are complimentary. We can supply alcoholic beverages such as beer and wine before and during your stay and these are charged at cost plus a 10% handling fee. We have a wine list available in the villa or choose your own wines from our below ground wine cellar. You’re are obviously free to bring your own.

Groceries bought on your behalf also incur a 10% handling fee and are delivered to the villa. You may also choose to do your own grocery shopping. We keep all receipts for your reference.

Transport

We’ll organize transport to and from town, or any other place you might want to go to. We’ll make sure you have a courteous and safe driver to take you to wherever you please. Transport fees are charged at cost and very reasonable. We can also assist you with car & motorcycle rentals.

Please allow us to pick you up from the airport. This will allow for a smooth transfer as our villa isn’t easy to find. This is a complimentary service. Before you arrive we’ll ask you to share your flight details so we can whisk you away to the villa as soon as you arrive.

Additional costs

Non-alcoholic drinks, tea and coffee are complimentary and so are basic snacks. Alcoholic beverages are supplied at cost plus a 10% handling fee. Groceries are charged at cost plus a 10% handling fee if delivered to the villa. Please enquire about the services of a private chef. All transportation is charged at cost. All fees are added to your final bill.

Reservations & Bookings

Please use the booking form on this site make a reservation request. We will get in touch with you as soon as we receive the request. Once the booking has been confirmed a 10% deposit is due to hold the booking. This deposit is payable via credit card, Paypal or direct bank transfer to our Thai account. The balance is due 7 days before arrival at the villa. Upon departure, the villa manager will present you with your final bill, which is payable by credit card or cash.

Your Hosts

Three Kings Villa is owned and managed by Roger & Nooch. Roger has been a professional diver all his life and Nooch is a former Police Officer. Together they form the team that makes Three Kings Villa tick. They also own and operate Kavil Guesthouse in the old town.
